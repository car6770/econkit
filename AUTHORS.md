## About Authorship

EconKit is an open-source educational economics toolkit.

The project welcomes contributions in areas such as:

- economics examples
- documentation
- tests
- data-cleaning utilities
- beginner-friendly tutorials
- macroeconomic analysis workflows
- reproducible research tools

Contributors may be listed here when they make meaningful improvements to the project.
